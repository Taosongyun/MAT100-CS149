01Course Introduction

Course Description

This site is for MATH 100 / COSC 149.2 and COSC 49.02, Topics in Probability: Permutations, Probability, and Sorting, taught by Peter Winkler.

The course studies probabilistic and combinatorial structures behind permutations, cycles, sorting procedures, random processes, posets, and related algorithmic questions. The pages here are intended as review-oriented study materials rather than official course documents.

Official Course Website

For authoritative course information, assignments, schedule details, and official materials, use the Dartmouth course page: MATH 100 Spring 2026 official website.
